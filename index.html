How to Use Reframe

1

Upload Images

Click "Choose Images" and select one or more images from your device. All common image formats are supported.

2

Position Your Image

Zoom: Use your mouse wheel (or pinch on touch devices) to zoom in and out.
Pan: Click and drag the image to reposition it within the frame.

3

Frame Your Shot

The rectangular frame shows what will be exported. Position and zoom your image so the desired area fills the frame. The original image is never cropped - only the framed area is exported.

4

Navigate Between Images

If you uploaded multiple images, use the thumbnails below the editor or the Previous/Next buttons to switch between them. Each image keeps its own zoom and position settings.

5

Export

Click "Download this image" to export the current image, or "Download all" to export all images at once. Each export is exactly 3000√ó1800 pixels in JPEG format, optimized to be under 1 MB.
